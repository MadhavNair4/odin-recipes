Easy Red Velvet Cake

Description

This recipe is a quick and easy way to make a delicious red velvet cake which a hit with children and adults alike. It requires 15 minutes of prep time and an additional 25 minutes to bake the perfect cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 (18.25 ounce) package white cake mix
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package non-instant chocolate pudding mix
  • red food coloring, as desired
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Prepare cake according to package directions, substituting half of the water called for with buttermilk (approximately 1/2 cup). Stir in pudding mix and food coloring.

  3. Pour into cake pan(s) and bake according to package directions.
